Who We Are

More Than a Shelter —
A Family

Founded in 2018, WIN foundation is a registered NGO in Thirunavalluer, Tamil Nadu, dedicated to providing comprehensive care for abandoned, destitute, and homeless adults aged 18 and above who have no family support.

We believe that dignity is not conditional on age, background, or circumstance. Our residents are not just given shelter — they are given a home, a community, and a reason to thrive.

🏠

Safe Shelter

Fully furnished accommodation with private and semi-private rooms for 80+ residents at any time.

🩺

Healthcare

On-site medical staff, regular health check-ups, and partnerships with government hospitals.

📚

Skill Training

Vocational programs to help capable residents regain independence and dignity.

🤝

Community

Cultural events, celebrations, and counselling to build belonging and mental wellness.

Our Programs

What We Do Every Day

Our holistic approach addresses physical, mental, social, and economic needs of every resident under our care.

🍽️

Nutrition & Meals

Three nutritionally balanced meals daily, prepared in our in-house kitchen following dietary guidelines for elderly and differently-abled residents. Special diets provided on medical advice.

🏥

Medical & Mental Health

Resident doctor, weekly specialist visits, tie-ups with Gandhi Hospital and NIMHANS-affiliated counsellors. Free medicines via PM-JAY (Ayushman Bharat) for eligible residents.

🧵

Vocational Rehabilitation

Tailoring, candle-making, paper craft, and computer literacy programs for residents below 60, helping them rebuild self-reliance and confidence.

⚖️

Legal Aid & Documentation

Assistance in obtaining Aadhaar, voter ID, caste certificates, pension registrations, and legal representation through empanelled pro-bono lawyers.

🎭

Social & Cultural Programs

Weekly cultural events, festival celebrations, yoga sessions, and inter-home sports meets that bring joy, connection, and mental wellness to residents.

🔁

Reintegration Support

Where possible, we work to reunite residents with families or place them in independent living with follow-up support for up to 12 months.

Government Partnership

Backed by the State & Centre

WIN foundation operates under the regulatory oversight and financial support of the Government of Tamil Nadu Department of Women Development & Child Welfare, and receives Central Government funding under multiple schemes.

This public-private partnership ensures accountability, quality standards, and sustained funding for our operations — alloWINg us to focus on care rather than survival.

  • Registered under Tamil Nadu Societies Registration Act, 1975
  • 12A & 80G certified — donations are tax-exempt
  • Annual audit by state-appointed auditor
  • Monthly inspection by District Welfare Officer
  • Beneficiary of PM-CARES Fund during COVID-19
🏛

National Social Assistance Programme (NSAP)

Monthly pension grants for elderly residents under Indira Gandhi National Old Age Pension Scheme.

💊

Ayushman Bharat – PM-JAY

Health coverage up to ₹5 lakh/year per resident for secondary and tertiary hospitalization.

🏠

Integrated Home for Adults (IHA)

State scheme providing ₹1,500/month per resident for food, clothing, and maintenance since 2011.

📋

CSR Partnership Program

Recognized CSR partner under MCA portal — 10+ corporates contributing annually under Sec 135.
